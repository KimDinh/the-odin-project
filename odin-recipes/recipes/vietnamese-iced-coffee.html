Vietnamese Iced Coffee

Description

A strong Vietnamese iced coffee recipe with sweetened condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons (30 ml) Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons coarse ground Coffee (use a roast suitable for espresso, the grind the same as for a french press)
  • Hot Water (almost to a boil)
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Pour the sweetened condensed milk into an 8-10 oz glass.
  2. Remove the top screen from the coffee filter. Put the ground coffee in the filter, screw screen back on, compacting the grounds.
  3. Place filter on the glass with the sweetened condensed milk. Pour just enough hot water to cover the grounds and let sit for 30 seconds.
  4. Loosen the filter screen screw at least 2 full rotations. Pour hot water to top of filter, cover and let sit until water has gone all the way through filter (It should take about 5 minutes at a rate of 3-4 drips/sec. If it is faster, coffee's grind is too coarse. If slower, coffee's grind is too fine.)
  5. When water has passed though filter, remove filter from glass. Stir coffee and sweetened condensed milk together.
  6. Stirring coffee and sweetened condensed milk together
  7. Add ice and enjoy.
